about

Hi there! My name is Dan Susman. Pleasure. This is my website.

I am a recent graduate from Northeastern University, where I earned my BS in Computer Science. I'm a sponge for new thoughts, advocate for collaboration, and proponent of discipline and hard work. I believe in clear, concise documentation and rich learning materials for newly built libraries, languages, and tools.

I'm currently working at Ginger Labs, where I contribute to the famous note-taking app, Notability. Though this means I spend a lot of time working in Swift and Objective-C, I'm also big into programming languages, compilers, dev tools, and privacy. I've been playing around with Zig recently, exploring CLI application development and getting into the weeds of embedded software development again. You can check out what I've been up to on my GitHub. Since I think learning in public has its benefits, I tend to share my projects as public repos there.

If you're interested in working or chatting with me, please feel free to reach out!

work

I'm currently a Software Developer at Ginger Labs, doing all things iOS, macOS, iPadOS. More details to come, I'm sure.

Previously, I was a Software Engineering Co-op at Corindus Vascular Robotics, a Siemens Healthineers Company. There, I worked on a state of the art medical robotic devices R&D team. I focused on application and cloud development, integration testing, and embedded software. I also spent some time doing hardware stuff, building robots, no big deal.
